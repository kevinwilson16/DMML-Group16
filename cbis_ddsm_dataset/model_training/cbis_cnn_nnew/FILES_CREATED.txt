Input Size:          224√ó224√ó3 RGB images
Output:              2 classes (Benign, Malignant)
Trainable Params:    ~10M parameters
Optimizer:           Nadam (learning_rate=0.0001)
Loss Function:       Categorical Crossentropy
Batch Size:          32
Max Epochs:          50 (with early stopping)

    - AUC:        0.80-0.90
    - Precision:  70-80%

Training Time:
    - With GPU:   2-4 hours
    - With CPU:   15-20 hours

================================================================================
DATASET INFORMATION
================================================================================

Name:                CBIS-DDSM
Full Name:           Curated Breast Imaging Subset of DDSM
Total Images:        ~10,239 JPEG images
Total Cases:         ~6,775 cases
Participants:        1,566 patients
Train/Test Split:    ~80/20

Classes:
    - Benign (including BENIGN_WITHOUT_CALLBACK)
    - Malignant

Abnormality Types:
    - Calcification
    - Mass

Image Types:
    - Full mammogram images
    - Cropped ROI images
    - ROI mask images

Note: The training script uses cropped ROI images for better focus on 
      abnormalities, but you can configure it to use full mammograms.
